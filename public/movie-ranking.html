The Best Movies According to Brian

My top 3 movies So far.

Spirit: Stallion of the Cimarron

Uses very little narration with amazing music to really bring out the illustration of the story. Very deep for how little talking is involved.

Howls moving Castle

Great Artwork, adventurous story that brings stories from past present and future together smoothly

How to train a Dragon

Amazing soundtrack, Amazing animation, and a fun story.
